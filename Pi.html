Now this is the Raspberry-Pi. Created by the Raspberry Pi foundation, manufactured by Sony,
this line of computers are about the size of a credit card, smaller!
These things are hooked up with Broadcom prosesers (like on some phones!). The ports include USB(4), HDMI, Touch screen display port, Camera port, audio in/out,
mini USB (power).
